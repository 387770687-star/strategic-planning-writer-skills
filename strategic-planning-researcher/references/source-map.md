# Source Map

Use this map to match planning topics to sources.

## Company Context

Preferred sources:

- National PipeChina official website and official public accounts
- annual reports, ESG/social responsibility reports, public announcements
- SASAC information involving central SOEs
- user-provided internal materials

Use for:

- company positioning
- business scope
- pipeline and facility data
- reform progress
- safety, technology, and digitalization achievements

## Policy Context

Preferred sources:

- State Council
- National Development and Reform Commission
- National Energy Administration
- SASAC
- Ministry of Natural Resources, Ministry of Ecology and Environment, Ministry of Emergency Management as needed
- provincial governments and energy bureaus for regional plans

Use for:

- energy security
- modern energy system
- oil and gas market reform
- pipeline tariff and fair open access
- natural gas utilization
- storage and peak-shaving
- dual carbon and green transition
- strategic emerging industries

## Market and Industry

Preferred sources:

- National Bureau of Statistics
- National Energy Administration data
- China Natural Gas Development Report and similar official/industry publications when available
- IEA, Energy Institute/BP, major SOE public reports
- recognized energy research institutes

Use for:

- demand forecast
- supply structure
- import dependence
- price trend
- regional consumption
- benchmark comparison

## Project and Investment

Preferred sources:

- user-provided project tables
- approved planning documents
- public EIA/project approval announcements
- provincial major project lists

Use for:

- project route
- length, diameter, pressure, capacity
- investment amount
- completion schedule
- approval and construction status

## Source Reliability Labels

- A: official/current/user-provided primary source
- B: authoritative public report or recognized institution
- C: credible media/secondary source needing verification
- D: unverified or outdated reference; do not use for final claims

## Non-Official Sources — Do Not Use

The following categories of websites and platforms must NOT be used as data sources. Their content may only serve as a pointer to discover the original source:

**Commercial news portals**: qq.com (腾讯新闻), sina.com.cn (新浪新闻), 163.com (网易新闻), sohu.com (搜狐新闻), ifeng.com (凤凰网), thepaper.cn (澎湃新闻非政策解读类)

**Self-media / content platforms**: WeChat Official Accounts (微信公众号), Toutiao / Baijiahao (今日头条/百家号), Zhihu (知乎), Xueqiu (雪球), Weibo (微博), Xiaohongshu (小红书)

**Aggregator / wiki platforms**: Baidu Baike (百度百科) as sole source, Sogou Baike (搜狗百科), 360 Baike (360百科)

**Unofficial data aggregators**: Any page compiling statistics without citing an original authoritative source.

**Rule**: When data found on these platforms is needed, trace it back to the original issuing authority (government website, ministry website, central SOE official site, NBS, IEA, etc.). If the original source cannot be located, mark the data as `[待补充引用]`.

## Official Website Retrieval Priority

When the knowledge base lacks specific data, search in this order:

1. **政府网站** (.gov.cn domains) — State Council, NDRC, NEA, NBS, provincial governments
2. **央企官网** — National PipeChina, CNPC, Sinopec, CNOOC official sites
3. **国际组织官网** — IEA (iea.org), EIA (eia.gov), World Bank (worldbank.org), Energy Institute
4. **行业协会官网** — China Oil and Gas Industry Association, China Petroleum and Chemical Industry Federation
5. **央企年报/公告** — Annual reports, ESG reports, official announcements published on company websites or stock exchange filings

Each item retrieved from an official website must be recorded with:
- Full URL
- Retrieval date
- Publication date of the source document (if available)
- Cross-validation status (single source vs. multi-source confirmation)
