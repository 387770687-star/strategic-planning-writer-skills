# Source Map

Use this map to match planning topics to sources.

## Company Context

Preferred sources:

- National PipeChina official website and official public accounts
- annual reports, ESG/social responsibility reports, public announcements
- SASAC information involving central SOEs
- user-provided internal materials

Use for:

- company positioning
- business scope
- pipeline and facility data
- reform progress
- safety, technology, and digitalization achievements

## Policy Context

Preferred sources:

- State Council
- National Development and Reform Commission
- National Energy Administration
- SASAC
- Ministry of Natural Resources, Ministry of Ecology and Environment, Ministry of Emergency Management as needed
- provincial governments and energy bureaus for regional plans

Use for:

- energy security
- modern energy system
- oil and gas market reform
- pipeline tariff and fair open access
- natural gas utilization
- storage and peak-shaving
- dual carbon and green transition
- strategic emerging industries

## Market and Industry

Preferred sources:

- National Bureau of Statistics
- National Energy Administration data
- China Natural Gas Development Report and similar official/industry publications when available
- IEA, Energy Institute/BP, major SOE public reports
- recognized energy research institutes

Use for:

- demand forecast
- supply structure
- import dependence
- price trend
- regional consumption
- benchmark comparison

## Project and Investment

Preferred sources:

- user-provided project tables
- approved planning documents
- public EIA/project approval announcements
- provincial major project lists

Use for:

- project route
- length, diameter, pressure, capacity
- investment amount
- completion schedule
- approval and construction status

## Source Reliability Labels

- A: official/current/user-provided primary source
- B: authoritative public report or recognized institution
- C: credible media/secondary source needing verification
- D: unverified or outdated reference; do not use for final claims
