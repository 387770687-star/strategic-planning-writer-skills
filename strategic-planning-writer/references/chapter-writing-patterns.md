# Chapter Writing Patterns

Use these formulas to draft chapters without becoming generic.

## Development Foundation

Structure:

1. overall positioning and asset/business scope
2. achievements during the baseline period
3. capability foundation
4. unresolved constraints
5. strategic implication

Paragraph pattern:

`从总体看，...。围绕...，公司形成了...能力，支撑了...。同时也要看到，...仍存在...，主要体现为...。上述基础和短板共同决定了下一阶段必须...。`

Good content includes data on pipeline mileage, capacity, stations, storage, LNG, utilization, safety, investment, digital systems, market service, and reform progress. If unavailable, use placeholders.

## Situation and Environment

Use four layers:

1. policy and national strategy
2. energy supply-demand and security
3. industry reform and market mechanism
4. technology and low-carbon transition

Each layer should end with "implications for the group".

Pattern:

`从政策导向看，...正在由...转向...。这意味着管网企业不仅要承担...，还要在...方面形成更强支撑。对国家管网集团而言，关键在于...。`

## SWOT and Strategic Implications

Do not stop at listing.

After SWOT, add:

- SO strategy: use strengths to seize opportunities
- WO strategy: use opportunities to repair weaknesses
- ST strategy: use strengths to control threats
- WT strategy: avoid weak areas and manage risks

Keep SWOT concise in table form, then write 600-900 characters of strategic implications.

## Guiding Thought

One paragraph, solemn and compact. Recommended chain:

`political guidance -> national energy security -> modern energy system -> group mission -> main business quality improvement -> emerging business exploration -> safety bottom line -> development objective`

Avoid excessive slogans. Every major phrase should point to a later task.

## Principles

Write 5-8 principles. Each principle:

`坚持X。以...为导向，围绕...，重点...，推动...。`

Common principles:

- 坚持服务大局、保障安全
- 坚持全国一网、统筹布局
- 坚持市场导向、开放共享
- 坚持创新驱动、数智赋能
- 坚持绿色低碳、融合发展
- 坚持效益约束、价值创造
- 坚持底线思维、防控风险

## Objectives

Objectives must include:

- qualitative strategic positioning
- quantitative indicators if evidence is available
- stage goals: 2026-2027/2028, 2030, 2035 or user-specified periods
- capability goals: network, storage, dispatch, safety, digital, market service, innovation, organization

Use placeholders for missing targets:

`到2030年，天然气管网总里程达到[待补充]公里，管输能力达到[待补充]亿方/年，重点区域双气源保障能力明显提升。`

## Business Deployment

Each business section uses:

1. strategic role
2. market/resource basis
3. core problem
4. deployment path
5. projects/actions
6. expected value

Pattern:

`该业务是...的重要支撑。当前...呈现...趋势，但在...方面仍存在...。下一阶段应按照...思路，重点推进...。一是...；二是...；三是...。通过上述安排，形成...能力。`

## Investment and Value

Separate financial return, strategic value, public value, and risk. For uncertain data, do not create exact numbers.

Required parts:

- investment categories
- annual/phase rhythm
- funding logic
- value dimensions
- sensitivity factors

## Risk Analysis

Each risk uses two paragraphs:

1. risk manifestation, cause, probability/impact
2. mitigation measures, responsible mechanism, early-warning indicator

Common risks:

- policy and tariff risk
- demand and utilization risk
- resource supply risk
- engineering cost and schedule risk
- safety and integrity risk
- digital/cyber risk
- emerging business technology route risk
- investment return risk
- organization coordination risk

## Safeguard Measures

Each safeguard should not repeat tasks. It should provide implementation conditions:

- organization and coordination
- planning and investment discipline
- policy/government coordination
- technology and digital platform
- talent and incentives
- capital and financing
- safety/compliance/internal control
- monitoring, evaluation, and rolling adjustment

Pattern:

`围绕...，建立...机制。重点从...、...、...三个方面发力，形成...闭环，确保...。`
