# Word Budget

For Chinese reports, use "characters" rather than English word count.

## 22,000-Character Minimum Budget

| Chapter | Target Characters | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| Executive summary | 1,200 | optional but recommended |
| Development foundation | 3,000 | achievements + problems |
| Situation analysis | 3,800 | policy, market, reform, technology |
| Overall thinking and objectives | 2,500 | guiding thought, principles, indicators |
| Business deployment | 7,500 | core body of report |
| Investment/value/risk | 2,000 | may split if needed |
| Safeguards | 2,000 | mechanisms and resources |
| Outlook | 800 | concise ending |
| Total | 22,800 | safely above 20,000 |

## 28,000-Character Premium Budget

| Chapter | Target Characters |
|---|---:|
| Executive summary | 1,800 |
| Development foundation | 4,000 |
| Situation analysis | 4,800 |
| Overall thinking and objectives | 3,200 |
| Business deployment | 9,500 |

Use the premium budget when the user asks for "充分、深入、交付级".

## Expansion Methods

Add depth through:

- regional breakdown
- business-line breakdown
- stage goals
- indicator systems
- policy evolution
- market scenarios
- capability gaps
- project categories
- investment rhythm
- risk matrix
- implementation mechanism

Never expand by repeating policy slogans or restating the same task in multiple sections.
